RCB marketing head arrested, event firm officials detained over killer stampede.

Bengaluru police have arrested RCB marketing head Nikhil Sosale and detained three officials of event organising in connection with the stampede that killed 11 people. 

The action comes after the Karnataka government suspended top police officers amid criticism of mismanagement.
