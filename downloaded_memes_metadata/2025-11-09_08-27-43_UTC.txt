INDIA: In a heartwarming story of love and dedication, 22-year-old Kanpur paan seller Abhishek Yadav surprised his wife with a precious gift bought entirely from coins he had painstakingly saved. 

Working at his Pan stall, he collected every Rs. 10 and Rs. 20 coin he received, day after day, for over a year. His quiet perseverance culminated in two sacks filled with coins, totaling over Rs. 1 lakh. 

With this, he visited a jewellery shop in Ahirwan Raja Market and purchased a gold chain worth around $1.25 lakh, paying with the coins and arranging installments for the remainder.
